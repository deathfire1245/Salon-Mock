# **App Name**: EleganceHaven Salon

## Core Features:

- Static Content Pages: Display pre-defined content for Hero, About, Services, Gallery, and Testimonials sections.
- Client Testimonial Carousel: Showcase customer reviews in a rotating or swipeable format.
- Simple Contact and Booking Form: Allow users to submit their name, contact details, and requested service. For MVP, this data is not stored, just presented for form validation and potential email sending (front-end only).
- Social Media Integration: Links to the salon's Facebook and Instagram profiles.
- AI Style Advisor: A tool that recommends hairstyles, color options, or beauty treatments based on user-provided text descriptions (e.g., hair type, desired look, occasion). The AI will provide personalized suggestions with brief justifications.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme is light, reflecting serenity and natural beauty. Primary calls-to-action (CTA) color: muted rose (#C395B6). Background color: a very light, desaturated rose (#F9F0F6). Accent color: a richer, deeper plum (#992999) for highlights and interactive elements.
- Headlines will use 'Playfair' (serif, geometric, high-contrast) for an elegant, fashionable touch. Body text will use 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for modern warmth and excellent readability.
- Modern, minimalistic line icons for service listings and contact details to maintain a clean aesthetic. Social media icons should be standard, recognizable brand logos.
- Clean, spacious layout with ample white space to emphasize elegance. Content sections are clearly delineated, promoting intuitive navigation. The hero section uses full-width imagery with a prominent, centered headline and CTA.
- Subtle animations and transitions, such as smooth fades for section transitions, slight hover effects on interactive elements like buttons and service icons, and a gentle slide for the testimonial carousel. Focus on fluidity and refinement rather than jarring movements.
